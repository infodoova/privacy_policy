Privacy Policy for Doova AI Last Updated: January 3, 2026 Doova AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. Content Data: The images, videos (Reels), and captions you provide to our app for the purpose of publishing them to your Facebook Page. 2. Authentication: To maintain a secure connection between Doova AI and the Facebook API. We do not: Create or manage user accounts within our system. Sell or share your data with any third-party advertisers or data brokers. Use your content for any purpose other than publishing it to your designated Facebook Page. 3. Data Retention and Deletion Retention: We do not store your media (images/videos) after they have been successfully uploaded to Facebook. Page access tokens are stored only for the duration of your active session or as long as needed to fulfill scheduled posts. How to Host This on GitHub (For Free) To get a URL that Facebook will accept (e.g., https://yourname.github.io/doova-privacy/): Create a Repository: Go to GitHub and create a new public repository named doova-legal. Upload the File: Create a new file named index.html. Paste the Content: Paste the Privacy Policy text above into that file. (If using HTML, wrap the text in basic tags for better formatting). Enable GitHub Pages: Go to Settings (in your repo) > Pages (on the left sidebar). Under Build and deployment, set the source to "Deploy from a branch" and select main (or master). Click Save. Get the URL: GitHub will give you a link at the top of that page. This is the Privacy Policy URL you paste into the Facebook Developer Dashboard. 3.
