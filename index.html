Privacy Policy for Doova AI

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Doova AI ("we," "our," or "us") operates as a tool to help users publish content to Facebook. This policy outlines how we handle data via the Meta Graph API.

1. Data We Collect

We only access data necessary to post content to your Facebook Pages. This includes:

  • Public Profile & Page Info: To identify which Pages you manage.
  • Access Tokens: To stay authorized to post on your behalf.
  • Media Content: The images, Reels, and captions you provide for uploading.

2. How Data is Used

Your data is used strictly for content publishing. We do not use your information for marketing, nor do we track your activity outside of the posting process. We do not have a user account system; your identity is managed via Facebook Login.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your Facebook Platform Data with any third-party advertisers or data brokers.

4. Data Deletion & Revocation

Users can revoke our access at any time through their Facebook "Settings & Privacy" > "Apps and Websites" menu. To request the manual deletion of any cached metadata, please contact us below.
